# How to Export Your Netflix Viewing History

This guide walks you through exporting your Netflix watch history and uploading it to Popcorn.

## Step 1: Open Netflix in Your Browser

Go to [netflix.com](https://www.netflix.com) using a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.).
This does **not** work from the Netflix app on your phone or TV — you need a browser.

## Step 2: Go to Your Account Page

Click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then click **Account**.

## Step 3: Find Your Profile

Scroll down to the **Profile & Parental Controls** section.
Click on the profile whose viewing history you want to export.

## Step 4: Open Viewing Activity

Click **Viewing Activity** to see a list of everything you have watched.

## Step 5: Download Your History

Scroll all the way to the bottom of the viewing activity page.
Click the **Download All** link. This will save a file called `NetflixViewingHistory.csv` to your computer.

## Step 6: Upload to Popcorn

1. Open the Popcorn dashboard in your browser.
2. Click the **Upload** button.
3. Select the `NetflixViewingHistory.csv` file you just downloaded.
4. Wait for processing to complete — this may take a minute depending on how much you have watched.

## Step 7: Review Flagged Titles

After processing, some titles may need your help to match correctly.
If there are any, you will see them on the **Review** page. Just pick the right match from the options shown.

That's it! Your watch history is now in Popcorn and you will start getting recommendations and alerts.
